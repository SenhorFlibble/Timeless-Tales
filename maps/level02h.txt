Our decision to investigate the rumours of renegade minions has resulted in the discovery of a sizeable enemy present. Surprisingly, none of the local garrisons report any missing soldiers. This means that either the enemy forces have relocated here after defecting, or that they were never under our jurisdiction to begin with.

These revelations have lead to speculation that these rebels may in fact be coming through an as of yet undiscovered portal.

You are to take command of Dunath Outpost and see to its defences. Your first task shall be to assign a peasant to oversee the construction of a suitable barracks. Once it is complete you will be able to recruit five additional soldiers to help defend the position.
